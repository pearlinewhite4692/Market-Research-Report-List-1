<p><ul><li>Vacuum-Assisted</li><li>Fine Needle</li><li>Core Needle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle-based biopsy gun market comprises three main types: vacuum-assisted, fine needle, and core needle biopsies. Vacuum-assisted biopsy uses suction to extract larger tissue samples, offering greater efficiency and accuracy. Fine needle aspiration biopsy employs a thin needle to collect small tissue samples for cytological analysis, often used for initial evaluations. Core needle biopsy utilizes a thicker needle to remove a cylindrical sample of tissue, providing more detailed information for diagnosis, making it suitable for various clinical applications.</p></p>
