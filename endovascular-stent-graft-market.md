<p><p>The endovascular stent graft market comprises three main types: synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic. </p><p>Synthetic stent grafts are made from materials like polyester or polytetrafluoroethylene, offering durability and low thrombogenicity. Biological stent grafts utilize natural tissues or cells, promoting biocompatibility and reducing infection risk. Biosynthetic stent grafts combine synthetic materials with biological elements to enhance performance and integration with the body, providing a balance between strength and biological functionality. Each type serves specific clinical needs and patient conditions, influencing purchasing decisions in the market.</p></p>
