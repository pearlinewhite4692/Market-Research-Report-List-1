<p><p>Full Ice Protection Systems are crucial for aircraft safety, preventing ice accumulation on critical surfaces. The market includes **De-Icing Systems**, which remove ice after it forms, typically using pneumatic boots, electro-thermal heating, or chemical methods. **Anti-Icing Systems**, on the other hand, prevent ice formation altogether, often utilizing heated surfaces, bleed air, or fluid-based solutions. Both systems enhance flight safety and efficiency, with de-icing focusing on post-formation removal and anti-icing on proactive prevention, catering to diverse aviation needs.</p></p>
