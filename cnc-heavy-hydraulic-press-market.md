<p><p>The CNC heavy hydraulic press market comprises two primary types: horizontal hydraulic presses and vertical frame hydraulic presses. Horizontal hydraulic presses are designed for applications requiring wide workspace and efficient loading of large materials, making them ideal for metal forming and fabrication. In contrast, vertical frame hydraulic presses are suited for tasks that demand precision and compactness, often used in applications like stamping, molding, and assembly. Both types leverage advanced CNC technology to enhance efficiency and accuracy in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
