<p><p>The automotive gasoline fuel injection system market comprises several types. Single-Point or Throttle Body Injection uses one injector for multiple cylinders, while Port or Multipoint Fuel Injection features individual injectors for each cylinder, enhancing fuel distribution. Sequential Fuel Injection delivers fuel to each cylinder precisely when needed, improving efficiency. Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for better performance and emissions control. Other types may include emerging technologies or hybrid systems, catering to diverse automotive applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
