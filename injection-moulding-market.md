<p><p>Injection molding machines are categorized into Electric, Hybrid, and Other types based on their drive mechanisms. Electric machines use servo motors for precise, energy-efficient operations, ideal for high-precision applications. Hybrid machines combine hydraulic and electric systems, offering a balance of power, speed, and energy efficiency, suitable for versatile production needs. The "Other" market includes specialized or less common machines, such as hydraulic or all-electric variants with unique features, catering to niche applications or specific industry requirements. Each type serves distinct manufacturing demands.</p></p>
