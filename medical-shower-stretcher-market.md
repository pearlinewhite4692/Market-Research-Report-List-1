<p><p>The medical shower stretcher market is categorized into two main types: fixed height and adjustable height. Fixed height stretchers offer a stable, consistent platform for patient transport and hygiene routines, making them suitable for facilities with uniform construction features. In contrast, adjustable height stretchers enable caregivers to customize the stretcher height according to the patient's needs and the caregiver's comfort, enhancing accessibility and ease of use. Both types are essential for ensuring patient safety and facilitating efficient hygiene care in medical settings.</p></p>
