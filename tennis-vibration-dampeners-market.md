<p><p>The tennis vibration dampeners market features two primary types: “Button” dampeners and “Worm” dampeners. Button dampeners are small, circular devices that easily attach between the strings of a racket, reducing vibrations and providing a softer feel upon ball impact. Worm dampeners, resembling a slim, elongated shape, slide between the strings and can be adjusted for tension and placement, offering customizable vibration reduction. Both types enhance player comfort, improve control, and promote better performance during matches and practice sessions.</p></p>
