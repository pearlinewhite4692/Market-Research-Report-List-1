<p><p>The photosensitive solder resist ink market is divided into solvent-based and non-solvent-based types. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility but may pose environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Non-solvent-based inks, on the other hand, use water or other eco-friendly carriers, reducing environmental impact while maintaining high performance and durability. Both types cater to different manufacturing processes in electronics, impacting factors like application method, drying time, and overall production efficiency.</p></p>
