<p><p>The glycerol esters market is segmented into three primary types: monoglycerides, diglycerides, and triglycerides. Monoglycerides are single fatty acid esters, used in food and cosmetics for emulsification. Diglycerides contain two fatty acid chains, enhancing texture and stability in various applications. Triglycerides, the most complex, consist of three fatty acids and serve as energy storage in food products. Each type plays a crucial role in improving product quality, extending shelf life, and enhancing mouthfeel across diverse industries.</p></p>
