<p><p>The packaging checkweighers market is categorized into two primary types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously weigh products as they move along a production line, ensuring efficient throughput and immediate quality control. They are ideal for high-speed operations. Conversely, intermittent checkweighers pause the product for weighing, providing precise measurements for slower production processes. Both types enhance accuracy in packaging and compliance with weight regulations, playing a crucial role in industries such as food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
