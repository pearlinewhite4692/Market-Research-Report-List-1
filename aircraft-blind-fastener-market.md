<p><strong>The Aircraft Blind Fastener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drive-Pin</li><li>Break-Stem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft blind fastener market is categorized into two primary types: drive-pin and break-stem fasteners. Drive-pin fasteners utilize a pin-driven mechanism for securing two or more components, offering quick installation without requiring access to the backside of the material. Break-stem fasteners, on the other hand, feature a stem that breaks off once the desired tension is achieved, enabling reliable fastening in tight spaces. Both types play a crucial role in aircraft assembly, ensuring structural integrity and reducing weight.</p></p>
