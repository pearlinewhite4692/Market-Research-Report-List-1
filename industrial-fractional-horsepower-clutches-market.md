<p><p>The Industrial Fractional Horsepower Clutches Market encompasses various types, including overrunning clutches, electromagnetic clutches, and permanent magnet clutches. Overrunning clutches allow a driven component to rotate faster than the driving component, preventing damage and ensuring smooth operation. Electromagnetic clutches use electromagnetic force to engage and disengage power transmission, providing precise control. Permanent magnet clutches leverage magnetic fields for engagement, offering high efficiency and low power consumption. Together, these clutches enhance performance and reliability across various industrial applications.</p></p>
