<p><p>The epoxy film adhesive market includes two main types: flexible epoxy resin adhesive and waterborne epoxy adhesive. Flexible epoxy resin adhesives are designed to provide excellent flexibility and bonding strength, making them ideal for applications requiring durability, such as automotive and aerospace components. Waterborne epoxy adhesives, on the other hand, offer reduced volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and are environmentally friendly, catering to industries focused on sustainability. Both types are widely utilized in various sectors, including construction and electronics, for their superior adhesion properties.</p></p>
