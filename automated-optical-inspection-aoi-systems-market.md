<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Systems are advanced technologies used to detect defects in manufacturing processes, particularly in electronics. **2D AOI Systems** utilize two-dimensional imaging to inspect components, identifying issues like soldering defects, misalignments, or missing parts on flat surfaces. **3D AOI Systems** add depth perception, enabling precise measurement of height, volume, and complex shapes, making them ideal for inspecting intricate components with varying elevations. Both systems enhance quality control, with 2D focusing on surface-level defects and 3D offering comprehensive, multi-dimensional analysis for higher accuracy.</p></p>
