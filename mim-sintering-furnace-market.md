<p><p>The MIM (Metal Injection Molding) sintering furnace market comprises two primary types: batch furnaces and continuous furnaces. Batch furnaces process materials in discrete loads, allowing for greater flexibility and control over sintering conditions, making them ideal for smaller production runs. In contrast, continuous furnaces facilitate a constant flow of materials through the system, enhancing efficiency and scalability for high-volume applications. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs, catering to varying production capacities and operational strategies in MIM applications.</p></p>
