<p><p>The air conditioning connection components market encompasses two primary types: split components and cabinet components. Split components refer to systems where the condenser and evaporator are separated, allowing for flexible installation and efficient cooling. In contrast, cabinet components are self-contained, often integrated systems that house all necessary components within a single unit, suitable for limited spaces. Both types serve distinct applications in residential and commercial settings, addressing diverse needs for temperature control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
