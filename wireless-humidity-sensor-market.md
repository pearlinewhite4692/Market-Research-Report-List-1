<p><strong>The Wireless Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless humidity sensor market is primarily segmented into relative and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the percentage of moisture in the air compared to the maximum amount the air can hold at a given temperature, providing contextual data crucial for environmental control. In contrast, absolute humidity sensors quantify the actual mass of water vapor in a specific volume of air, offering precise moisture levels regardless of temperature. Both types facilitate applications in HVAC, agriculture, and smart building technologies.</p></p>
