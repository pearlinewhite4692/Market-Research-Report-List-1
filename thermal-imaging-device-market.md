<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Infrared Device</li><li>Mid-wave Length Device</li><li>Long-wave Length Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal imaging device market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave infrared devices. Short-wave length devices, utilizing wavelengths between 1-3 micrometers, excel in detecting high-temperature surfaces. Mid-wave length devices, operating from 3-5 micrometers, are versatile for various applications, including industrial and military use. Long-wave length devices, ranging from 8-14 micrometers, are ideal for detecting heat emissions in low-temperature environments, making them suitable for surveillance and building inspections. Each type serves distinct needs in diverse sectors.</p></p>
