<p><p>Shielded Type Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are categorized into single shielded and double shielded types, each designed for specific geological conditions. Single shielded TBMs feature a protective shield to support excavation while allowing for the installation of lining behind the cutter head. In contrast, double shielded TBMs provide additional support, incorporating a second shield that adds stability in particularly challenging environments. These machines enhance safety and efficiency in tunnel construction, addressing varying ground pressures and enabling effective segment installation.</p></p>
