<p><p>Intumescent coatings are specialized fire-protection materials that expand when exposed to heat, forming a thick insulating char that shields substrates from flames. The market includes two main types: cellulose-based and hydrocarbon-based. Cellulose coatings derive from natural plant materials and are known for their eco-friendliness and effectiveness in passive fire protection. Hydrocarbon coatings, on the other hand, are petroleum-derived and offer superior durability and resistance to harsh environments. Both types are essential for enhancing fire safety in various structural applications.</p></p>
