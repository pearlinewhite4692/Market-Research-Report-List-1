<p><p>The Thick Film Thermal Printhead market comprises two primary types: Corner Type and Side Face Type. Corner Type printheads use a configuration where heating elements are positioned at the corners, enhancing thermal efficiency and reducing overall size. In contrast, Side Face Type printheads feature heating elements aligned on the sides, providing a wider printing area and better heat distribution. Both types cater to various applications, including label printing and POS systems, driven by demands for precision, speed, and reliability in thermal printing technology.</p></p>
