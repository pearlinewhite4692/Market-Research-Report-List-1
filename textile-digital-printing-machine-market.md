<p><p>The textile digital printing machine market comprises three main types: Sublimation Inkjet Printing, Direct to Fabric Printing, and Direct to Garment Printing. Sublimation Inkjet Printing transfers dye onto fabric using heat, ideal for polyester materials and vibrant designs. Direct to Fabric Printing directly applies ink onto textiles, suitable for various fibers and producing high-quality outputs. Direct to Garment Printing focuses on printing designs directly onto garments, primarily used for short runs and customized items, enhancing creativity and reducing waste in production.</p></p>
