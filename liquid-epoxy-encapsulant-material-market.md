<p><p>The liquid epoxy encapsulant material market encompasses various types, including liquid molding compounds, capillary underfills, and non-conductive pastes. Liquid molding compounds are used for encapsulating electronic components, providing mechanical support and environmental protection. Capillary underfills are designed for filling gaps between chips and substrates, enhancing thermal and mechanical stability. Non-conductive pastes serve to secure components while preventing electrical conductivity, making them ideal for applications where insulation is critical. Together, these materials cater to diverse industrial encapsulation needs.</p></p>
