<p><strong>The Steering Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Sensor</li><li>Contacting Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steering Position Sensor market includes two main types: magnetic sensors and contacting sensors. Magnetic sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect the position of the steering wheel, offering high durability and resistance to wear. In contrast, contacting sensors rely on physical contact to measure the steering angle, providing precise readings but may experience wear over time. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and performance by enabling advanced driver assistance systems and improving steering responsiveness.</p></p>
