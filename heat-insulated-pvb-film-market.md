<p><ul><li>Endothermic Film</li><li>Reflective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat insulated PVB film market comprises two main types: endothermic and reflective films. Endothermic films absorb heat through chemical reactions, effectively reducing interior temperatures and enhancing energy efficiency. In contrast, reflective films utilize special coatings to bounce solar radiation away, minimizing heat absorption and glare. Both types serve various applications, including automotive glazing and architectural building envelopes, providing comfort and energy savings while contributing to sustainability by reducing reliance on air conditioning and heating systems.</p></p>
