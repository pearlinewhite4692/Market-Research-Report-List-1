<p><p>TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System) valves are critical components integrated into vehicle tires to monitor and transmit real-time tire pressure data to the driver. These valves ensure optimal tire performance, enhance safety, and improve fuel efficiency by alerting drivers to under or over-inflated tires. TPMS valves are available in two types: direct TPMS, which uses sensors to measure pressure, and indirect TPMS, which relies on wheel speed sensors.</p><p>The TPMS Valves Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing vehicle safety regulations, rising consumer awareness about tire maintenance, and the growing adoption of advanced automotive technologies. The focus on sustainability and energy efficiency is also driving innovation in TPMS valve designs, ensuring their compatibility with modern vehicles.</p></p>
