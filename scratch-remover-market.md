<p><ul><li>Solid Type</li><li>Emulsion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scratch remover market is primarily segmented into two types: solid and emulsion. Solid-type scratch removers typically come in paste or wax forms, offering concentrated abrasion for deep scratches and providing a protective layer after application. In contrast, emulsion-type scratch removers are liquid solutions that combine with water, making them easier to apply and ideal for minor surface scratches. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with solid types favored for heavy-duty use and emulsions for convenience and quick touch-ups.</p></p>
