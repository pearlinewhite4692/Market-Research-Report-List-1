<p><strong>The Inks for Planographic Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Inks</li><li>Solvent-Based Inks</li><li>UV-Cured Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inks for planographic printing are categorized into three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and ideal for high-quality prints, as they dry quickly and reduce harmful emissions. Solvent-based inks offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various substrates, though they may have higher VOC emissions. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light to instantly cure the ink on contact, providing vibrant colors and exceptional resistance to fading and chemicals. Each type serves specific printing needs.</p></p>
