<p><strong>The HVAC Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Sensors</li><li>Absolute Pressure Sensors</li><li>Gauge Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC pressure sensors market includes three main types: differential pressure sensors, absolute pressure sensors, and gauge pressure sensors. Differential pressure sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, crucial for monitoring air flow and filter performance. Absolute pressure sensors gauge pressure relative to a vacuum, used for accurate measurements in controlled environments. Gauge pressure sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly utilized in HVAC systems to ensure optimal functioning and safety in various applications.</p></p>
