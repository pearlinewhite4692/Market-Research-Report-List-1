<p><p>Robotic palletizers are essential in material handling, categorized into full-automatic and semi-automatic systems. Full-automatic palletizers operate independently, utilizing advanced robotics to manage the entire palletizing process, minimizing human intervention and maximizing efficiency. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, streamlining operations and enhancing productivity while addressing varying budget and labor requirements.</p></p>
