<p><p>Balloon ablation catheters are medical devices used in procedures like ablation for cardiac arrhythmias. The market is segmented into two main types: Regular Type and Pressure Monitoring Type. Regular Type catheters are designed for straightforward ablation procedures, focusing on effective tissue destruction without real-time feedback. In contrast, Pressure Monitoring Type catheters incorporate technology to monitor pressure during ablation, providing enhanced safety and precision. This differentiation caters to varying clinical needs and improves patient outcomes in electrophysiological treatments.</p></p>
