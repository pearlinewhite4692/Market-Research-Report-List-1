<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry utilizes various pump types for fluid handling and processing. Centrifugal pumps are widely used for their efficiency in transferring large volumes of liquids. Positive displacement pumps provide precise flow control, ideal for viscous materials. Jet pumps utilize fluid dynamics to create a vacuum, suitable for moving small quantities. Air lift pumps use compressed air to lift liquids, often in wastewater applications. Other specialized pumps cater to unique pharmaceutical needs, enhancing production efficiency and ensuring product integrity.</p></p>
