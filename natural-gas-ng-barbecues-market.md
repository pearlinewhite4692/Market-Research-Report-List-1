<p><p>The Natural Gas (NG) barbecues market is divided into two main types: self-contained devices and embedded devices. Self-contained devices are portable and include their own gas tank and ignition system, making them easy to use in various outdoor settings. Embedded devices, on the other hand, are built into outdoor kitchens or designated spaces, requiring a direct connection to a natural gas supply. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with self-contained models emphasizing convenience and embedded types focusing on a permanent, integrated setup.</p></p>
