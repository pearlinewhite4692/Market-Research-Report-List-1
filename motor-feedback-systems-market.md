<p><p>Motor feedback systems encompass two primary market types: rotary and linear. Rotary feedback systems are used in applications requiring precise position and speed control in motors that rotate, often seen in robotics, CNC machines, and automation. Conversely, linear feedback systems are designed for applications that require linear motion control, commonly found in conveyor systems, elevators, and industrial machinery. Both types provide critical information to enhance performance, accuracy, and efficiency in various industrial and commercial automation applications.</p></p>
