<p><p>The commercial vehicle foundation brakes market comprises three key types: S-Cam drum brakes, disc brakes, and wedge brakes. S-Cam drum brakes utilize a camshaft to force brake shoes against a drum, providing reliable stopping power for heavy vehicles. Disc brakes use a rotor and caliper system, offering superior heat dissipation and performance, while wedge brakes operate by inserting wedges to apply force, ensuring effective braking. Each type serves distinct applications, reflecting varying performance needs and design preferences in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
