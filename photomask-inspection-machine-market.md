<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Inspection Machine market features two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects individual dies against one another, ensuring consistent quality across produced masks by comparing them directly. In contrast, the DB method evaluates a die against a stored database of verified designs, allowing for quick identification of defects and deviations. Both methods are vital for maintaining the integrity and precision of photomasks in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
