<p><strong>The Carbon Black Pelletizing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pelletizing</li><li>Dry Pelletizing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon black pelletizing equipment market is divided into two main types: wet pelletizing and dry pelletizing. Wet pelletizing involves using moisture to facilitate the agglomeration of carbon black, resulting in consistent pellet size and improved handling properties. In contrast, dry pelletizing utilizes mechanical pressure without additional moisture, producing pellets that are denser and suitable for various applications. Both methods aim to enhance the usability and efficiency of carbon black in industries such as rubber, plastics, and coatings.</p></p>
