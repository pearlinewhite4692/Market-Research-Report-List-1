<p><ul><li>Anionic</li><li>Nonionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Synthetic Associative Thickener market is primarily divided into two types: anionic and nonionic thickeners. Anionic thickeners, which are negatively charged, are commonly used in water-based formulations and enhance viscosity while providing stabilization in paints, coatings, and personal care products. Nonionic thickeners, on the other hand, lack a charge and are preferred for their compatibility with a broader range of ingredients, making them ideal for sensitive formulations in various applications, including cosmetics and household products.</p></p>
