<p><strong>The Aircraft Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Angular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft position sensor market is categorized into two main types: linear and angular sensors. Linear position sensors measure the displacement of components along a straight path, essential for monitoring mechanisms like landing gears or control surfaces. Angular position sensors, on the other hand, detect the rotational angle of components, crucial for systems such as flight control surfaces and engine positioning. Both types play vital roles in enhancing aircraft safety, precision, and efficiency, catering to specific operational requirements in aviation.</p></p>
