<p><p>The Construction Mapping Service market encompasses two main types: Aerial Surveying and Terrestrial Surveying. In contrast, Terrestrial Surveying utilizes ground-based methods, such as GPS, theodolites, and laser scanners, to gather precise measurements and details directly from the site. Both methods play crucial roles in enhancing project accuracy, planning, and management in construction and engineering projects.</p></p>
