<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small wind turbine market is primarily divided into two types: Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs feature blades mounted horizontally and are more common, often used for their higher efficiency and better performance in high wind conditions. VAWTs, on the other hand, have blades that are vertically positioned, providing advantages like a lower center of gravity and less noise, making them suitable for urban areas or residential applications. Both types cater to varying energy needs and installation contexts.</p></p>
