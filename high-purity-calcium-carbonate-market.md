<p><ul><li>Uncoated Precipitated Calcium Carbonate</li><li>Coated Precipitated Calcium Carbonate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high purity calcium carbonate market comprises two main types: uncoated and coated precipitated calcium carbonate. Uncoated precipitated calcium carbonate (PCC) is characterized by its high purity and is primarily used in applications requiring superior brightness and low impurity levels, such as in pharmaceuticals and food products. In contrast, coated PCC is treated with fatty acids to enhance its hydrophobic properties, making it suitable for applications in plastics, coatings, and paper, where enhanced performance and compatibility are essential.</p></p>
