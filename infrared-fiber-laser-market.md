<p><p>The infrared fiber laser market is primarily segmented into two types: continuous wave (CW) and pulsed (impulse) lasers. Continuous wave lasers emit a constant beam, making them ideal for applications requiring steady energy output, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, impulse type lasers release energy in short bursts, allowing for precise control and high peak power, suitable for engraving and medical procedures. Each type serves distinct industrial and commercial needs, adapting to varying application demands.</p></p>
