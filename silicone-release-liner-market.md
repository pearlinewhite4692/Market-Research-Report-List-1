<p><p>The silicone release liner market is divided into two main types: paper and film. Paper liners, often made from bleached or unbleached substrates, are cost-effective and widely used in applications like labels and tapes. Film liners, typically made from materials like polyester or polypropylene, offer enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and a smoother surface, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as medical devices and adhesive products. Both types serve to facilitate the easy release of adhesives or coatings.</p></p>
