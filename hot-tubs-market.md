<p><p>The hot tub market primarily consists of two types: acrylic and fiberglass. Acrylic hot tubs are molded from a durable, non-porous material, offering vibrant colors and extensive customization options. They typically provide better insulation and require less maintenance. In contrast, fiberglass hot tubs are pre-manufactured from a single piece of reinforced fiberglass, making them lightweight and easy to install. While they are sturdy and resistant to cracks, their design options may be more limited compared to acrylic models.</p></p>
