<p><p>The indexable cutting tools market features several material types, each with distinct properties for machining applications. Cemented carbides, or tungsten carbides, offer high hardness and wear resistance, making them ideal for metal cutting. Cermets combine ceramics and metals for improved toughness. Ceramics are used for high-speed machining due to their hardness but are brittle. Cubic boron nitride (cBN) and polycrystalline cBN (PcBN) are preferred for hardened steel, while diamond tools excel in cutting non-ferrous materials due to their exceptional sharpness and durability.</p></p>
