<p><ul><li>Plasma Lighter</li><li>Electric Arc Lighter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric lighter market comprises primarily two types: plasma lighters and electric arc lighters. Plasma lighters utilize high-voltage electric arcs to create a flameless spark, making them windproof and eco-friendly. Electric arc lighters, on the other hand, generate a concentrated arc of electricity that ignites materials quickly. Both types offer advantages over traditional lighters, such as rechargeable batteries and increased safety features, catering to environmentally conscious consumers and those seeking reliable, durable ignition tools for various applications.</p></p>
