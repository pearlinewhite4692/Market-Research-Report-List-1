<p><p>The Continuous Rotary Extrusion Equipment market features two primary types: Curvilinear Type and Radial Type continuous extrusion machines. Curvilinear Type machines focus on producing complex cross-sectional shapes through a continuous feeding process, ideal for materials requiring precise geometries. In contrast, Radial Type machines utilize a radial flow mechanism, ensuring uniform material distribution and enhanced strength in extruded products. Both types cater to various industries, including aerospace and automotive, by improving efficiency and material properties while streamlining production processes.</p></p>
