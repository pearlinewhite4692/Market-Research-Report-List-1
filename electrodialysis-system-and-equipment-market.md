<p><p>The electrodialysis system and equipment market is segmented into three primary types based on membrane types: heterogeneous membranes, homogeneous membranes, and bipolar membranes. Heterogeneous membranes, often used for salt separation, have distinct layers with varying properties, enhancing efficiency. Homogeneous membranes feature uniform material composition, providing consistent ion transport and durability. Bipolar membranes facilitate the electrochemical conversion of ions, enabling acid-base reactions. Each type serves unique applications in water desalination, chemical processing, and food industries, contributing to advancements in separation technologies.</p></p>
