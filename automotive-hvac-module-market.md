<p><p>The Automotive HVAC module market is primarily categorized into two types: Direct Blowing Type and Duct Type. The Direct Blowing Type HVAC system delivers conditioned air directly into the cabin, providing quick temperature adjustment and improved efficiency. In contrast, the Duct Type system uses a network of ducts to distribute air evenly throughout the vehicle, enhancing comfort for all passengers. Both types are designed to optimize climate control, but they differ in their air distribution mechanisms and application suitability.</p></p>
