<p><p>The snow goggles market is categorized into three main types: ordinary lenses, myopic lenses, and presbyopic glasses. Ordinary lenses provide standard visibility and protection against snow glare. Myopic lenses are designed for individuals with nearsightedness, enhancing focus for those who need prescription corrections while enjoying winter sports. Presbyopic glasses cater to older adults who require multifocal lenses, accommodating both distance and near vision. Each type meets specific visual needs, ensuring enhanced performance and safety in snowy environments.</p></p>
