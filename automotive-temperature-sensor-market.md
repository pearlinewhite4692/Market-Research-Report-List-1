<p><strong>The Automotive Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouple Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Temperature Sensor Market includes Thermistor and Thermocouple Temperature Sensors, both crucial for monitoring and regulating vehicle temperatures. Thermistor sensors, known for high sensitivity and accuracy, use resistance changes to measure temperature, ideal for engine coolant and cabin climate systems. Thermocouple sensors, durable and wide-ranging, generate voltage based on temperature differences, suited for exhaust and high-temperature applications. Both types enhance vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency by ensuring optimal thermal management in automotive systems.</p></p>
