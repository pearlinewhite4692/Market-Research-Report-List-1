<p><p>Walk-behind trowels are essential tools in construction for finishing concrete surfaces, available in two main types: hydrostatic and mechanical. Hydrostatic trowels use hydraulic systems for smoother operation and enhanced control, providing consistent power for larger areas. In contrast, mechanical trowels rely on a gearbox and should be manually operated for precision in smaller spaces. Both types cater to varying project needs, offering durability and efficiency for finishing tasks while allowing operators to achieve high-quality concrete surfaces.</p></p>
