<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blower</li><li>Positive-Displacement Blower</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blower markets are categorized into three main types: centrifugal blowers, positive-displacement blowers, and others. Centrifugal blowers utilize rotating impellers to generate airflow, ideal for high-volume applications. Positive-displacement blowers compress air through a mechanism that traps air and forces it out, suitable for conveying materials at consistent volumes. The "other" category includes specialized blowers like regenerative and rotary lobe blowers, each designed for specific uses, such as vacuum creation or high-pressure applications, enhancing operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
