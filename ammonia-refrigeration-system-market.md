<p><p>The ammonia refrigeration system market includes various types such as evaporator units, industrial racks, compressors, and heat exchangers. Evaporator units facilitate heat absorption, cooling stored products efficiently. Industrial racks are multi-compressor setups that enhance capacity and efficiency for large-scale refrigeration needs. Compressors are crucial for circulating ammonia through the system, ensuring continuous operation. Heat exchangers transfer heat between fluids, improving overall system performance and energy efficiency. Together, these components play vital roles in industrial refrigeration applications.</p></p>
