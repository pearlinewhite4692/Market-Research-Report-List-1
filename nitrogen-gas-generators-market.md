<p><p>The nitrogen gas generators market primarily consists of two types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) and membrane nitrogen generators. PSA systems utilize adsorbent materials to separate nitrogen from air under pressure, providing high-purity nitrogen production suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, membrane nitrogen generators employ selective permeation through polymer membranes to separate nitrogen from other gases, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution for lower purity requirements. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and electronics.</p></p>
