<p><strong>The UV Cure Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV cure printing inks are specialized formulations that harden when exposed to ultraviolet light. The market is primarily divided into two types: arc curing and LED curing. Arc curing utilizes traditional mercury vapor lamps, generating intense UV light suitable for high-speed applications but involves higher energy consumption and heat. In contrast, LED curing employs light-emitting diodes, offering lower energy requirements, reduced heat output, and longer lifespans for the light sources, making it increasingly popular for various printing applications.</p></p>
