<p><p>The capillary rheometer market is characterized by two main types: single-barrel and multi-barrel. Single-barrel rheometers utilize a single channel for measuring flow behavior, ideal for simpler applications and materials. In contrast, multi-barrel rheometers feature multiple channels, allowing for simultaneous testing of various materials or conditions, enhancing efficiency and data collection. Both types are crucial for assessing the viscosity and flow properties of polymers and other materials, aiding industries in quality control and material development.</p></p>
