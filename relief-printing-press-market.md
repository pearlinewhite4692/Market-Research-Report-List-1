<p><ul><li>Traditional Relief Printing Press</li><li>Flexographic Printing Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The relief printing press market is primarily divided into two types: traditional relief printing presses and flexographic printing presses. Traditional relief printing involves processes like letterpress and woodblock printing, where raised surfaces transfer ink to paper. In contrast, flexographic printing uses flexible plates made from rubber or photopolymer, allowing for high-speed printing on various substrates, including packaging materials. Both methods serve distinct purposes, with traditional relief often used for art and limited runs, while flexographic is favored for high-volume commercial printing.</p></p>
